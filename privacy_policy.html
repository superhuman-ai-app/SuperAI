Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Super AI is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection in compliance with GDPR and international privacy standards.

By using our anticipatory design platform and adaptive systems, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy. We prioritize intelligent minimalism in data collection, gathering only what's essential for delivering seamless interaction experiences.

Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide our proactive intelligence services and enhance human-technology symbiosis. This includes account details (name, email), usage patterns for cognitive flow optimization, and technical data for system performance.

Our adaptive systems may collect interaction data to improve anticipatory design features. All collection is transparent, purposeful, and aligned with delivering superhuman email experiences and future interface capabilities.

Cookies Usage

We use essential cookies to maintain session integrity and functional cookies to remember your preferences for seamless interaction. These enable our platform to deliver personalized cognitive flow experiences without compromising your privacy.

Analytics cookies help us understand usage patterns and optimize our speed aesthetics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some features may require essential cookies to function properly.

Analytics & Performance

We employ privacy-respecting analytics to measure platform performance and user engagement with our digital intuition features. This data is aggregated and anonymized, helping us refine anticipatory design elements and adaptive systems.

Performance monitoring ensures our intelligent minimalism approach delivers optimal speed aesthetics. No personally identifiable information is shared with third-party analytics providers without explicit consent.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user of Super AI's proactive intelligence platform, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal data and understand how it's processed
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten) under specific circumstances
  • Right to restrict processing of your data
  • Right to data portability in machine-readable format
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Our adaptive systems are designed with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring data protection is integral to our future interface development.

While we maintain robust security protocols, no system is completely impervious. We continuously monitor and update our security practices to address emerging threats and maintain the integrity of your data within our human-technology symbiosis ecosystem.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Super AI does not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in delivering our anticipatory design features, but only under strict confidentiality agreements and GDPR compliance requirements.

Any data sharing is limited to what's necessary for service delivery, such as cloud infrastructure providers or email delivery services for superhuman email functionality. We ensure all partners maintain equivalent privacy standards.
